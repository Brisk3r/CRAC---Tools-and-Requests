VISION BOARD 01

The Path to the Podium

Content Marketing Strategy

"The Path to the Podium" Storytelling Series

High-quality narratives capturing the diverse journey of our community. From competitive youths to seniors in rehabilitation, we showcase personal milestones that define our facility.

Impact

Deep Emotional Resonance

Channel

Web & Social Video

Identify Champions

Profiling youth swimmers, seniors, and adult learners to reflect our diverse user base.

Production Quality

Partnering with local videographers for authentic "day in the life" storytelling.

Targeted Launch

Strategic distribution via website and newsletters with localized ad spend.

Digital Innovation

"Morning Laps" Virtual Leaderboard

A digital-physical hybrid gamification strategy. Patrons log weekly distances via QR codes, creating organic "bragging rights" and driving retention.

  • Increases repeat visitation
  • Rich demographic usage data
  • Local business sponsorship integration

Next Step: Secure local café sponsor for weekly "Most Improved" rewards.

VISION BOARD 02

Community Gamification

B2B Partnership Program

Local Sports Club Recovery

Formalized outreach for regional sports teams—football, netball, and cricket. Tapping into heated pools for professional-grade post-match hydrotherapy and injury prevention.

Active Recovery Pitch

A structured pitch deck highlighting hydrotherapy benefits for injury prevention, specifically tailored for regional club presidents and coaches.

The "First Session Free"

Low-barrier entry for the top five local clubs to experience the facilities firsthand, creating immediate buy-in and relationship building.

Club Pass Billing

Moving away from individual entries to a seasonal subscription model, ensuring guaranteed revenue and high-volume off-peak usage.
